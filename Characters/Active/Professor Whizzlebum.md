## Description
Small, round, and perpetually soot-stained. Talks too fast, forgets what he’s saying halfway through, and somehow manages to blow something up at least once a day.

Travels with a mechanical pigeon named Percival who’s _probably_ smarter than he is.

---

## Clichés

### Inventor (Level 4/4)
**Tools of the Trade**  
- A multi-pocketed lab coat full of unidentifiable gadgets  
- Goggles with multiple flip-down lenses (most cracked, some tinted weirdly)  
- A collapsible workbench that unfolds from a suitcase  
- Percival, the mechanical pigeon — doubles as a recorder, scout, and sometimes a flamethrower  

**Likely Abilities**  
- Design and build improbable contraptions on the fly  
- Reverse-engineer or jury-rig devices from scraps  
- Recognize and exploit weaknesses in mechanisms  
- Convincingly declare disasters as “intentional prototypes”  

### Absent-Minded Professor (Level 2/3)
**Tools of the Trade**  
- A stack of half-filled notebooks with no consistent numbering  
- Ink-stained fingers and a perpetually misplaced pen  
- A head full of brilliant, half-remembered theories  

**Likely Abilities**  
- Spout obscure scientific trivia or academic jargon at will  
- Accidentally stumble onto "genius-level" solutions  
- Forget key details in hilarious or inconvenient ways  

### Tinkering Gadgeteer (Level 1/2)
**Tools of the Trade**  
- Tiny screwdriver set and magnifying monocle  
- Percival’s toolkit hidden in his chest compartment  

**Likely Abilities**  
- Repair or upgrade any mechanical device (with varying success)  
- Create quick mechanical distractions or traps  
- Fine-tune or sabotage clockwork, steampunk, or magitech mechanisms  

### Tea Enthusiast (Level 1/1)
**Tools of the Trade**  
- A dented silver teapot with scorch marks  
- Ceramic cup that says “World’s 3rd Best Genius”  

**Likely Abilities**  
- Brew restorative or mood-improving tea under any conditions  
- Impress or distract with perfect tea ceremony etiquette  
- Use tea as an impromptu solvent, polish, or cooling agent
